About the 1994 weekly

We spend an unhealthy time on the internet and we decided to do something about it. Actually, we decided to do something because of it. We created a fun and not-entirely-devoid-of worth place where people could unwind, kill some time on this weird place we called the internet, and hopefully learn something useful or at least not super shitty at the same time.

The Internet is coming. And it's going to rule your face!

Are you and your family ready?

Beavis and Butthead discover internet porn for the very first time.

Are you sick of real life? Ready for something new? There are about to be a couple billion people living on the inside of this big ass computer you are staring at and you can talk to all of them. Don't see what you are looking for? Don't fret, you can always try the catfish.

There is nothing any of us can do. We must all succumb. Your business, your friends, your kids, none of them will ever be the same. You cannot unsee the things you will see on the World Wide Web. But alas, within the pages of this digest we will teach you to be an internet warrior, a true nerd (this is a good thing). We will give you the knowledge and skills to make best use of this crazy new medium. We will help make your things go viral (also a good thing) and make sure your business doesn't go ratchet (a bad thing).

Here is a fantastic article written in December of 1994 to get you started. It's about how wacky the internet is with its emails, chat rooms, newsgroups, and links. What's next, chat roulette?

On a serious note, the internet might be the human race's most amazing invention. And it is strangeness, weirdness, and amazingness is all due to the fact that it really is a mirror of our collective unconscious. The internet is truly all connected, by links, in a way that as humans I think we only envision. And sometimes we need to protect that.
